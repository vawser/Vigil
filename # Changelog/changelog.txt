- Fixed a bug that caused the Assassin's Crimson/Cerulean Dagger +1, +2 and +3 effects not to occur properly.

- Thumb Ring of Pharris

    - +0 Effect: Increases arrow shot count by 1, but reduces maximum HP by 20%.
